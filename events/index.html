Events

Senior Week is filled with many events to celebrate our achievements. There are many different types of events for seniors to take part in. Some events have a limited number of spots and will require you to lottery in, while others are open to the whole class. The lottery is available as part of the registration form and is due on April 10 at 5 PM. You can view more info about the lottery on the Lottery page. You can access the registration form on the Registration page.

Senior Week is from May 31 to June 5, 2019. A complete schedule of events will be available after the lottery closes. You may view the currently planned events below. Prices, dates, events, and details are subject to change.

The Senior Week Committee is also responsible for distributing tickets to Tech Night at the Pops, an event sponsored by the Alumni Association. This event will be held after Senior Week and Commencement. More information about the event and distribution will be available soon.

Please be advised that events, locations, ticket counts, prices, and details are subject to change, even after the lottery closes.

Ticket sales for events will begin on April 12, following the release of lottery results. No student has a spot in an event until they have purchased their ticket. The deadline to purchase tickets is April 19, 2019. You will be able to purchase tickets by following instructions on the Registration page when sales begin.
